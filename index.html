The Problem

The Pentagon can't see what's inside its own drones

GAO-25-107283 found DoD supply chain mapping provides "little insight" into the vast majority of suppliers. Real crises have already proven the cost.

REAL EVENT · OCT 2024
Skydio Battery Crisis
China sanctioned America's largest drone maker. Battery supplier Dongguan Poweramp cut supply overnight. Skydio rationed batteries for months — $1.2B in bookings at risk.
1 supplier · 0 alternatives mapped
ACTIVE RISK
Gauntlet Concentration
25 vendors selected to build 200,000+ drones. Radio module supplier Doodle Labs serves 7+ competing teams. One failure cascades across the entire program.
7+ vendors · 1 radio supplier
STRUCTURAL
Rare Earth Chokepoint
China controls 85% of rare earth production and 90% of processing. Every drone motor uses neodymium magnets. April 2025 export controls directly threatened Western defense.
